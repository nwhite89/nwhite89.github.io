The ParaScroll format is a cross-platform display ad, which will display a particular creative depending on the type of the device allowing the creator of the advert to set a creative for Mobile devices and another for Desktop devices. When on Mobile the creative will display full width of the browser whereas the desktop creative will be full width of the article. The biggest part of this format is that the the creative will look at the entire page to look for the article on the page and place the creative within the Article looking for a paragraph which will allow for only 50% (maximum) of the creative to display on load and then add to the Article in this place.

Similar to the parallax creative it will start to scroll it's content once the first 50% of the creative is in view of the users browser and finish once the last 50% is in view. The intimediary stage between both top 50% and bottom 50% of the creative being in view, will scroll either faster or slower than the users scroll depending on the amount of content within the creative and the users window height. The creative will ensure that 100% of the content within the creative is viewed by speeding up or slowing down.
