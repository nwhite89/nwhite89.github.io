The Parallax format is generally a display ad however, it can launch into another second stage creative such as a lightbox. The idea of the Parallax format is to provide more content than a static image could display within whichever ad slot size.

Whilst a user scrolls a pages content the Parallax creative will start to scroll it's content once the first 50% of the creative is in view of the users browser and finish once the last 50% is in view. The intimediary stage between both top 50% and bottom 50% of the creative being in view, will scroll either faster or slower than the users scroll depending on the amount of content within the creative and the users window height. The creative will ensure that 100% of the content within the creative is viewed by speeding up or slowing down.
